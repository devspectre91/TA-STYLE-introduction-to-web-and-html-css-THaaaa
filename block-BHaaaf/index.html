The Wolverine

Logan, commonly known as the wolverine is the most fierce mutant from X-men series. He has retractable claws and an amazing quality to heal himself which gives him superiority over other mutants. At one point in his life, he participated in an experiment by William Stryker which involved putting admantium in his skeleton. He successfully recovered from that experiment. Now with admantum in his skeleton he was indestructible.
